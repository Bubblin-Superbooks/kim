Chapter 15

I'd not give room for an Emperor—
I'd hold my road for a King.
To the Triple Crown I'd not bow down—
But this is a different thing! I'll not fight with the Powers of Air—
Sentry, pass him through! Drawbridge let fall—He's the Lord of us all—
The Dreamer whose dream came true!
