Chapter 4

Good Luck, she is never a lady,
But the cursedest quean alive,
Tricksy, wincing, and jady—
Kittle to lead or drive.
Greet her—she's hailing a stranger! Meet her—she's busking to leave! Let her alone for a shrew to the bone
And the hussy comes plucking your sleeve! Largesse! Largesse, O Fortune! Give or hold at your will.
If I've no care for Fortune,
Fortune must follow me still!

The Wishing-Caps.
